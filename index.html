About Our Institution

The Neotia University is a UGC-approved private university in West Bengal, established under the West Bengal State Government University Act, 2014, and guided by Padmashree Harshavardhan Neotia of the AmbujaNeotia Group. The university is known for industry-focused education, modern infrastructure, and strong placement support.

  • UGC Approved & Government Recognized University
  • ICAR, PCI, BCI, INC & DG Shipping Approved Courses
  • 600+ Industry Connections for Internships & Placements
  • 600+ Industry Connections for Internships & Placements
  • Industry-Oriented Curriculum with Mandatory Internships
  • UG, PG & Doctorate Programs Across Multiple Disciplines
  • 50-Acre Wi-Fi Enabled Modern Campus
  • Hostel, Sports, Gym, Swimming Pool & Student Clubs
  • Safe, Secure & Ragging-Free Campus Environment
